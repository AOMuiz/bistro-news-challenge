Low and Slow

posted by BGB,November 15, 2016

This week i am exremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking,you submerge the food(usually vaccum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target tempreture you want the foodto be cooked to. In his book, cooking for geeks,Jeff potter describes it asultra-low temperature poaching.

Next month,we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a seat at the chef table, contact us before november 30.
